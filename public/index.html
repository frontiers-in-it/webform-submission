COMPX500: Frontiers in Information Technolgy

Welcome to COMPX500, a course for the Master of Technology Innovation in Business (MTIB) degree, where we explore cutting-edge advancements in the field of Information Technology. This course is designed for graduate-level students with a non-technical background who are eager to dive into the dynamic world of IT, focusing on the development and prototyping of innovative products. Throughout this course, you will gain hands-on experience in emerging technologies and learn how to apply these skills to real-world problems.

Through lab exercises you will:

  • Master AI Algorithms: Learn to apply and adapt existing AI algorithms to solve IT problems.
  • Explore Cybersecurity: Delve into the world of cybersecurity to understand how this field is shaping the future.
  • Design User-Centric Interfaces: Develop skills in user-centered design to create IT solutions that meet user needs effectively.

This website is in fact an integral part of the lab exercises. One of its functions is to provide some test pages for the cybersecurity component of the course, allow you to gain a better understanding of how data is transmitted between your browser and a web server.
